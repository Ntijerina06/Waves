What Are We?

Waves is more than an app. It’s a movement. We blend personal growth with community support and societal advancement. We empower individuals to set goals, share their journeys, and harness the power of community to create ripples that lead to global change.

Noah

Most of my life I was a watcher that sometimes did. This all went away in college, no I was not a D1 athlete. Then, as if it was hidden from me my entire life, I finally saw what was right in front of me, my ability to learn and grow. Now, I understand the way to build the bridge and walk the steps to the top of our metaphoric mountains. The mission remains a personal one. I want to be the best I can be. I strive for that Hole-in-One, even if that means I will be short of perfection every time; it is the mindset that drives me toward my ultimate goal, more birdie putts (excellence). This is my mission, to aim for the best version of myself, to transform that imaginative idea of what I can experience… into a reality.

Noah is one of the co-founders of Waves. He is an aspiring software engineer, currently studding cognitive science at De Anza College. Noah dedicated to building tools that help people grow and contribute to their communities.
